    • Ice Cream Cakes

    Need a special treat for a party, event or just because? J. Foster can help. Our indulgent ice cream cakes are the ultimate alternative to conventional cakes and pies, made in-house in delicious flavor combinations, packed to-go! J. Foster ice cream cakes turn any event into an elegant celebration.

    Here are some of the cakes that are ready to go:

    The Kids Supreme – Six layers of ice cream, crushed Oreos & chocolate chip cookies, Cookie Monster ice cream, crushed chocolate chip cookies, Oreo ice cream, Oreo cookie layer, Cookie Dough ice cream, whipped cream topping, rainbow sprinkles around the outside

    The Mudslide - Kahlua Heath Crunch ice cream, fudge layer, Chocolate Mousse ice cream, and chocolate whipped cream topping

    The Oreo - Oreo ice cream, Oreo cookie layer, Vanilla Chocolate Chunk ice cream, whipped cream topping surrounded with white and dark chocolate shavings and garnished with Oreos.

    The Standby – vanilla and chocolate ice cream with a homemade hot fudge layer.

    Novelties

    We make all of our novelties by hand in our shop in Avon. We start by baking fresh chocolate chip cookies for our sliders and J-Wich ice cream sandwiches and then we stuff them full of our delicious ice cream. Sliders are dipped in chocolate and J-Wichs are rolled in mini chocolate chips. The result is a treat voted number one cookie by the CT Specialty Food Association. We also bake our own brownies for our oversized brownie ice cream sandwiches. Carrot cake lovers will enjoy our own baked carrot cake sandwiches packed with our fresh cream cheese icing ice cream. If you love Oreo’s our giant Oreo sandwiches are sure to please. You can eat them filled with our sinfully delicious Oreo ice cream or your favorite J Foster flavor.

    We also make specialty ice cream bars such as toasted almond and chocolate dipped. If you are lactose intolerant or just feel like fruit, you’ll enjoy bars made out of our refreshing sorbets.
