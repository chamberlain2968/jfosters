    Gelato & Sorbetto

    Gelato is the Italian word for ice cream, so you could argue that gelato is ice cream with romance, but there’s a big difference between the soft, smooth texture and clean taste of gelato and the American-style heavier, richer textured ice cream. If you have ever been to a popular gelatoria in Italy you know what we are talking about. At J. Foster we have eliminated the extravagant cost of traveling to Italy to enjoy authentic gelato, because we have been trained by one of Italy’s premier gelato chefs.

    Sorbetto Flavors

    The base of all of our sorbets is water, sugar and corn syrup. These ingredients are combined to make a very smooth base. We then pack the base with fresh fruits and chocolate. All of our fruit sorbets are naturally no fat and dairy and gluten free. Our chocolate sorbets may contain some diary, depending on the chocolate used.
